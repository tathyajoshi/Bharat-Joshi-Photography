About Us

We offer professional photography services and are based in Mumbai since last 35 years. Our forte is Wedding photography and pre-weddings shoots, however our team is keen and perceptive in all types of events- Corporate events, Fashion, Still life, Interiors, advertising, Personal (birthday, anniversary, portfolio) call it anything and our lenses will be ready for you. We aim to breathe in sheer splendour to your auspicious wedding day. Our passion for wedding photography is grounded on pure joy of celebrating love, which is the foundation of all weddings. As Candid wedding photographers our approach is to capture the 'real life' fleeting moments which will eventually turn into 'most memorable moments of your life'. You can blindly trust us to capture those timeless moments – emotions – moods – drama – colours – to tell the story of your D-day. No tricks, no gaudy effects, nothing too posed or awkward postures. We create images that will recreate the magic of your Big-day every time you see them. Apart from regular photography, we offer products and services such as HD Videos and Photo-book. We cover events in Maharashtra and All Over India*, depending on the requirement. We are comfortable communicating in English, Marathi, Hindi and Gujarati.
